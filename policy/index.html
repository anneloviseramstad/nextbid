Privacy Policy

At NextBid, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Account details and auction activity
  • Payment and billing information
  • IP address and browser/device information

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Facilitate bidding, sales, and purchases
  • Process payments securely
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send updates, promotions, and service notifications

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to personalize content and ads, analyze traffic, and understand how users interact with our platform. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with third parties only to:

  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Deliver essential services through trusted partners

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@nextbid.com
