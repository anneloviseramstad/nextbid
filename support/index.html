Support Team

At NextBid, we’re committed to providing you with the best experience possible. If you have questions, issues, or need assistance, you’re in the right place. Check out the topics below or reach out to our support team directly.

Common Questions

  • How do credits work? Credits are the currency you use to place bids on items. You can purchase credits in various packages on your account page.
  • How do I place a bid? Simply choose an item you want, use your credits to place a bid, and watch the timer. The highest bid when the timer runs out wins the item.
  • What happens if I win an auction? Congratulations! You’ll receive an email with payment and delivery instructions.
  • Can I get a refund? Please review our Policy for details about eligibility and procedures.
